Befit

This website has the main idea of ensuring the health of people and providing them an online platform to maintain themselves. This project tells them their current health status, guides their daily eating habits and updates them about their exercise routines. It saves the time delay in scheduling a doctor's appointment as one of it's module focuses on finding the nearby doctor and scheduling the appointment or consultancy online. This website also aims on generating some specific diet charts corresponding to some main diseases and keeping the user updated about their current treatments available. The Diet Planner is a complete package for leading a healthy lifestyle.This site provides combine information of all diet related issues at one place. For that user needs to create an account by giving their details like name, email id and password. When user successfully logs in his account, the website will ask for his basic details like height and weight to calculate user’s BMI indicating the user’s present condition. Now if user falls into the alarming categories then he needs to work on his diet. Then the user will be asked further details like diseases any, eating habits (veg or non-veg),blood group, daily workout,etc…so that website know his daily calorie requirements. And finally the website generates a unique diet chart fulfilling the users’ requirements. The registered user can also get the information about doctors at the desired location. Also user has the facility to contact with doctors just by entering their queries in “contact us” field. The doctors are available as admins from where they can reply to user. In this way the website provide easy interaction between user and doctors.
